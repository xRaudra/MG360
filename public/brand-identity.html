Our Mission

Why MedGlobal360 Exists

To make world-class medical care accessible, affordable, and
fully coordinated for every patient — wherever they are in the world.

Millions of patients travel across borders each year seeking treatments they cannot access at home — but the journey is fragmented, opaque, and stressful. MedGlobal360 exists to change that: one app, one timeline, one trusted partner from first consultation to full recovery.

🔍
Access

Surface JCI and NABL accredited hospitals and verified specialist doctors across India — all in one place, searchable by treatment, specialty, location, and cost. No gatekeepers, no brokers.

⚖️
Transparency

Show real treatment pricing, success rates, and patient reviews side by side. Every cost is visible before a patient commits — no hidden fees, no surprises, no markups passed from hospitals to patients.

🧭
Journey

Own the full care journey — not just the booking. From consultation and visa guidance to flights, accommodation, and post-treatment follow-up, every step is tracked, documented, and coordinated inside the app.

Patient-First, Always

Every feature is built around the patient experience — not hospital interests or commission structures. Revenue follows value delivered to patients, not the other way around.

Mobile-Native by Design

Built as a mobile app from day one — not a web portal with an app bolted on. Real-time journey tracking, instant notifications, and document management require a native mobile experience.

Africa-First Positioning

The Africa-to-India corridor is the highest-demand, lowest-served route in medical tourism. MG360 is built natively for this corridor — not as an afterthought through a travel partnership.

Trust Through Accreditation

Every hospital and doctor on the platform is verified against JCI and NABL accreditation standards. Patients see real ratings, real reviews, and real credentials — not curated marketing copy.

Problem 01

Medical Information is Hidden, Fragmented, and Broker-Controlled

Patients seeking treatment abroad face a market designed against them. Pricing is opaque, accreditation is unverified, and the only guides available are commission-hungry brokers who charge 15–25% markups passed silently to the patient.

  • The same cardiac surgery can cost 3× more depending on which broker a patient contacts
  • No single platform surfaces verified JCI/NABL accreditation alongside real patient reviews
  • Patients cannot compare hospitals side by side — they depend entirely on what brokers choose to show them
  • Success rates and complication data are never disclosed in the traditional broker model
How MG360 Solves It
A Transparent, Verified Marketplace — No Brokers, No Markups

MG360 surfaces every hospital and doctor with full JCI and NABL accreditation status, real treatment pricing, verified patient ratings, and success rate data — all side by side. Patients see everything upfront and choose freely. Revenue comes from hospital partnerships, never from markups on patient care.

Verified JCI/NABL listings Transparent pricing Real patient reviews Side-by-side comparison Zero broker commissions
02
Problem 02

The Care Journey Falls Apart the Moment Booking is Confirmed

Medical travel involves over 15 distinct touchpoints — consultation, visa application, flights, accommodation, pre-op tests, hospital admission, surgery, recovery, and follow-up. Today, patients manage all of this alone across WhatsApp groups, scattered emails, and paper documents. Families have no visibility. Coordinators are unreachable. Documents get lost.

  • Patients miss critical follow-up appointments because no one is tracking them
  • Medical records and visa documents are spread across phones, email, and physical folders
  • Families back home have no real-time visibility into their loved one's status
  • Post-treatment care falls through the cracks once the patient returns home
How MG360 Solves It
End-to-End Journey Tracker — Every Step, One App

MG360 owns the full care journey inside the app. A real-time timeline tracks every milestone from first consultation through post-treatment follow-up. A document vault stores medical records, visa docs, and test results. Family sharing gives loved ones live visibility. In-app chat connects patients directly with their coordinator — no WhatsApp, no lost emails.

Real-time journey timeline Document vault Family sharing In-app coordinator chat Post-treatment follow-up
03
Problem 03

Africa's $2B Medical Tourism Corridor Has Zero Digital Infrastructure

Sub-Saharan Africa has 1.3 doctors per 1,000 people — less than a third of the WHO benchmark of 4.45. Advanced cancer care, complex cardiac surgery, and specialist orthopedics are simply unavailable across most of the continent. Over $2 billion in annual medical travel flows from Africa to India alone — yet not a single purpose-built digital platform exists for this corridor. Patients navigate entirely through informal networks and word-of-mouth, with no safety net.

  • Patients have no way to verify hospitals or doctors before travelling thousands of kilometres
  • Visa guidance for Indian medical visas is fragmented and country-specific — no single source of truth
  • Language barriers and time zones make coordination with Indian hospitals nearly impossible
  • Existing global platforms treat Africa as an afterthought — no local language support, no corridor-specific guidance
How MG360 Solves It
Built Natively for the Africa-to-India Corridor

MG360 is the first platform designed from the ground up for African patients travelling to India. It provides local-language support, Africa-specific Indian medical visa guidance, and a vetted network of India's top hospitals serving African patients. Community-based trust — verified reviews from other African patients — replaces the informal word-of-mouth networks that patients currently depend on.

Africa-first UX Multi-language support Medical visa guidance African patient community reviews India-specialist hospital network
